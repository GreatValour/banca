Frequently asked general questions

What is Banca doing to be a responsible lender?

Banca understands your concerns as a consumer and the importance of responsible lending. We abide by the applicable laws and regulations in those states in which we operate. Customers with credit difficulties should seek credit counseling.

What are the benefits of using Banca?

We're glad you asked. At Banca, you can expect:

  • Quick Loan Approval*
  • Outstanding Personal Service
  • A Simple Process for Quick Money
  • A Secure, Reliable and State-Licensed Lender or Arranger
  • Quick Help for Life’s Emergencies and Confidential 24/7 Online Convenience

Additional information may sometimes be necessary for approval. NV residents: verification of income is required by state law.

How long will it take to know if I'm approved?

Loan approval is quick.* Simply fill out the 3-step application and you’ll know your approval status in moments.

At times additional information may be necessary to complete the application. Applicants from Nevada and Washington are required by state law to present certain income verification during the application process, which may lead to some minor delays.

How do I receive the money?

Your Banca loan proceeds will be directly deposited to your checking account on the day you select in the application process (subject to governing state rules). When you sign your loan contract, you authorize Banca to direct deposit your requested loan amount into your checking account.
